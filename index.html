Why Chamonix?

An Outdoor Enthusiast's Dream

From skiing to hiking to enjoying gorgeous views of the Alps, there’s something for everyone in Chamonix, France.

In the summer you can hike from mountain hut to mountain hut, take in the soaring views from atop some of France’s highest peaks, and easily day trip to Switzerland or France. In the winter you can ride trams, gondolas, or chairlifts to some of the best skiing in the world, adventure into the backcountry, and access a dozen different ski areas on one pass. And all year round, you have access to a town full of world-class dining, shopping, and nightlife.

Places in Chamonix

Aiguille du Midi

Aiguille du Midi is a mountain peak in the Mont Blanc massif. It is 3,842 meters (12,605 feet) tall. A cable car to the summit was first constructed in 1955, and it held the record for the highest cable car in the world for about 20 years.

74400, Chamonix, France

Parc de Merlet

Come and watch the mountain animals roam freely in this animal park in a magical setting, at an altitude of 1500m, just opposite Mont Blanc.

Species to discover: ibex, sika deer, llama, mouflon, marmot, chamois and fallow deer!

Address

2495 Chem De Merlet, 74310 Les Houches
